Urban Research Foundation (URF) is a creative research based collaborative working in the domain of Urban Planning and Design and based in Aurangabad, Maharashtra.

URF is striving to bring the aspect of innovative participatory planning practices to the upcoming Tier-II and Tier-III cities of India. We envisage a sustainable urban development that is citizen centric in its approach and participatory in its nature. Our vision is to enable Tier-II and Tier-III cities of India in becoming livable, inclusive and vibrant urban centers that provide socio-cultural opportunities to its citizens. We also aim to steer these cities into alternative economic engines that can share the urban pressure of Tier-I cities.
